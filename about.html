About Charity

Every child deserves to be truly heard

Charity Lujan is a licensed and ASHA-certified Speech-Language Pathologist with over two decades of experience supporting children, families, and educational teams across North Texas. After 20 years with the Denton County Special Education Co-Op — serving Aubrey ISD, Sanger ISD, and Pilot Point ISD — she brings seasoned clinical depth and a genuine commitment to each child she works with.

Her preferred areas of practice center on school-aged children and adolescents with speech and language disorders, including social communication differences, articulation, fluency, and receptive and expressive language. She also offers enrichment programs for children without a diagnosis who are ready to build strong oral language and vocabulary skills before formal reading begins.

Through Meridian, Charity offers flexible, family-centered therapy that fits your life — including virtual sessions, in-home visits, daycare settings, and appointments at the Aubrey area library.
