The Meridian Approach

We don’t believe in one-size-fits-all therapy. Every child is different — their strengths, their challenges, their personality, and the way they learn. That’s why we take the time to truly understand your child before creating a plan.

Strengths-based
Building on what your child already does well
Neurodiversity-affirming
Respecting and supporting how your child experiences the world
Individualized
Therapy that adapts to your child, not the other way around
Practical
Focused on real-life communication, not just clinic tasks

“Meeting your child exactly where they are — and helping them grow from there.”
