Common questions

Before you reach out, a few answers

Do you accept insurance?

Meridian is a private-pay practice. A superbill can be provided upon request for families who wish to seek reimbursement from their insurance provider.

What ages do you serve?

Charity works with pediatric clients, with a focus on school-aged children with speech and language disorders.

Does my child need a diagnosis to receive services?

Not at all. Language enrichment and literacy programs are available without the need for an evaluation. For other services, an evaluation may be recommended to determine your child's strengths and needs and to develop a custom program.

How do I get started?

Fill out the referral form above — it takes about 3–5 minutes and gives Charity everything she needs to reach out and discuss next steps with your family.
